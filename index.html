ABOUT US

kira esports is india’s renowned professional gaming organization established in 2019. since our grassroots beginnings as fortnite professionals, we have grown into multifaceted professional gaming organization made by sportsmen, for the sportsmen.

with over 50+ regional, national and international championship, kira esports is synonymous with success. with its own rainbow six siege esports team standing currently at #29th in the world and the only team from south asia to be at this stage. in april 2021, kira esports defeated the japanese powerhouse “cyclops athlete gaming” and shocked the world as being the underdogs from south asia beating the best of the best from asia pacific.

more than just the esports team, kira esports is also a management enterprise. starting with our community, we have expanded into other areas within the gaming ecosystem.

gg squad

kira’s “gg squad” is a rapidly growing digital talent management brand that directly targets gaming influeners, streamers, and esport teams.

We believe "Life's better Multiplayer" and it is our goal to connect gamers everywhere through the creation and curation of viral content specifically made to "tag the squad in". gg squad is a platform where the gaming influencers meet with like minded brands with similar vision.

We’ve signed a stable of the best emerging talent in the space who output daily creative content around popular video games and pop culture trends.
